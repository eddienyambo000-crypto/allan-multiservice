# Allan Multiservice Group — Brand Guidelines

> Owner brand colors win on any conflict. These are the locked values.

## Positioning
**Rwanda's first verified, all-in-one marketplace for property, land & cars.**
Two-sided: we convert buyers (browse → WhatsApp/inquire) AND sellers (list with us → we move it).
Tone: confident, plain-spoken, trustworthy. Category leader, not a classifieds board.

## Typography
- **Display / headings:** Space Grotesk (500–700), tracking `-0.03em`, line-height `1.05`.
- **Body:** Inter (400–600), line-height `1.7`.
- **Labels / eyebrows:** JetBrains Mono, uppercase, tracking `0.16em`, sky color.

## Style direction — Premium Minimalism
- Oversized headings, generous whitespace, high contrast on white.
- Cards: 18px radius, 1px `--color-line` border, layered color-tinted shadow (never flat `shadow-md`).
- Motion: animate `transform` + `opacity` only; spring easing `cubic-bezier(0.16,1,0.3,1)`. No `transition-all`.
- Every clickable element: hover + focus-visible + active states; `cursor-pointer`.
- Icons: SVG only (Lucide-style). No emoji as icons.
